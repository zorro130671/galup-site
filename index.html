Since Day One

Our Story

Walking Alongside Women Others Have Left Behind

GalUP — Gals Up — was founded on a simple belief: that incarcerated and formerly incarcerated women deserve more than a system that forgets them the moment they leave.

We show up — with meals, mentorship, health education, and vocational training — building genuine relationships that last far beyond prison walls. Because lasting change only happens when people feel truly seen.

Today, GalUP runs seven community programs, partners with over 24 organisations, and is backed by a community of donors who believe in second chances.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Two commitments that guide every decision we make, every program we run, and every relationship we build.

Our Vision

A Singapore where every woman — regardless of her past — is truly seen, genuinely supported, and given the space to flourish. Where the measure of justice includes the healing it makes possible, not only the punishment it delivers.

Our Mission

To provide incarcerated and formerly incarcerated women in Singapore with holistic, sustained support — through nourishment, health, leadership, and skills — so that every woman has what she needs to write a new chapter on her own terms.

The Need

The Reality Few Talk About

Behind Singapore's reputation for safety is a group of women whose stories rarely surface in public conversation — women who are mothers, daughters, and caregivers, facing near-impossible odds after release.

Without the right support, the gap between incarceration and a stable, meaningful life on the outside is wider than it should be. Housing instability, employer stigma, and unaddressed trauma compound to make reintegration feel impossible for many.

GalUP exists to change this. From inside the walls to years after release, we walk with women every step of the way.
